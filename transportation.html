Getting to Cornell University

  • Closest Airport: Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport (ITH: Ithaca, NY)

    • Distance from Cornell Campus: 10 minutes by car (also accessible by TCAT Bus)
    • Recommended for: attendees traveling from states outside the Northeast/New England region (i.e. Florida, Midwestern states, etc.)
    • Most local Ithaca hotels have shuttle services from Ithaca Tompkins Regional Airport to the hotel, but you will have to contact the hotels to arrange your pickup.

  • Other Nearby Airports:

    • Syracuse Hancock International Airport (SYR: Syracuse, NY)
      • Travel time to Ithaca: 1-1.5 hours by car/bus
    • New York City & New Jersey Airports:
      • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR: Newark, NJ)
      • LaGuardia Airport (LGA: Queens, NY)
      • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK: NYC)
      • Travel Time to Ithaca: 4-6 hours by bus/car
    • Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF: Buffalo, NY)
      • Travel time to Ithaca: 2.5 hours by car, 3-4 hours by bus

  • Recommended for: attendees traveling from NYC, NJ, upstate NY, New England, Pennsylvania, Mid-Atlantic Region)

  • Short Line Bus (operated by Coach USA)

    • Departs from major cities in the Northeast to Ithaca
    • Stops at: Ithaca Bus Terminal, Collegetown (located directly South of Cornell Campus), Cornell North Campus, Cornell West Campus
    • Approximate price from NYC to Ithaca: $50 one-way. Student discounts available.

  • OurBus (for a referral code with a $10 discount, visit the OurBus website at https://ourbus.com/refer)

    • Departs from NYC to Ithaca, also with routes between DC & NYC
    • Stops at: Green Street in Downtown Ithaca
    • Approximate price from NYC to Ithaca: $25-30 one-way

  • Greyhound Bus

    • Departs from major cities to Ithaca Bus Terminal
    • Stops at: Ithaca Bus Terminal, 10-minute drive from campus
    • Approximate price from NYC to Ithaca: $50 one-way

  • Amtrak Train Service

    • Closest Amtrak stop: Syracuse, NY
      • Travel time from Syracuse Amtrak to Ithaca: 1-1.5 hours by car/bus Ithaca Airline Limousine can provide shuttle transportation from the train station to Ithaca. Contact Ithaca Airline Limousine directly for services.
      • Recommended for: attendees traveling from Syracuse, Northeast/New England Region

  • On-Campus Parking

    • After 5PM on weekdays and all day on weekends, you may park in permit-restricted spaces displaying only your municipal placard or license plate. Please adhere to time restrictions noted in areas.

  • Street Parking

    • The parking rate is $1.50 per hour in Ithaca city.

  • Downtown Parking

    • Downtown Ithaca parking is FREE on evenings and weekends at the meters and at the Seneca and Green St. garages.

  • Parking Garages

    • Seneca Garage: situated at the corner of Tioga and Seneca Streets across from the Ithaca Commons and Seneca Place/Hilton Hotel. This 450 car garage is operated by the City of Ithaca.
    • Green St. Garage: located on E. Green St., after the Cinemapolis marquee. This 400 car garage is operated by the City of Ithaca.
    • Cayuga Garage: located across from the Hotel Ithaca at the corner of Clinton and Cayuga Streets. This 700 car garage is privately managed by Allpro Parking.

If you have booked hotel rooms at Hotel Ithaca or Ithaca Marriott Downtown in the Commons, we have arranged a free shuttle service with Swarthout Coaches that will loop from Hotel Ithaca & Ithaca Marriott Downtown in the Commons to Cornell University Central Campus, on Friday evening and Saturday.
